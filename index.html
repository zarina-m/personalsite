about

I'm a web developer based in Toronto and a recent graduate of both Juno College (HackerYou) and the University of Toronto. My priorities as a programmer are straightforward, with accessible, reliable, and succinct code setting the bar for how I measure my success as a developer. In my pre-webdev life I utilized my English degree to copyedit and contribute to academic literature, fine-tuning my attention to detail in the process. I wholeheartedly believe in the Oxford comma and I have a penchant for (unapologetically) writing in all lowercase.
